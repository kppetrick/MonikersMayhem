7. **Alfa AWUS036ACM USB Wi-Fi Adapter**
   - **Price:** $35–$45

### Total Estimated Cost: ~$232–$287

## Why This Setup?

- **Pi 5 (8GB)**: Future-proof for ML/AI features, better performance headroom
- **Argon ONE V3 NVMe Case**: Premium look, excellent cooling, NVMe support for faster storage
- **NVMe SSD**: Much faster than microSD for OS and application storage
- **USB WiFi (Alfa)**: Essential for 4+ simultaneous player connections, better range and reliability
- **8GB RAM**: Extra headroom for Node.js + React app and future features

## Notes

- All prices are approximate and may vary by retailer
- Check availability at official Raspberry Pi retailers
- May need additional accessories for initial setup (keyboard/mouse temporarily)
